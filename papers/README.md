# TrialMCP Research Papers

Three research papers presenting TrialMCP from complementary perspectives, published under DOI [10.5281/zenodo.18870961](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.18870961).

## Papers

| Paper | Perspective | Audience | PDF |
|-------|-------------|----------|-----|
| **A** | [Clinical Operations](ClinOps/) | Sponsors, CRO leadership, trial ops directors, site networks | [trialmcp_clinops.pdf](ClinOps/trialmcp_clinops.pdf) |
| **B** | [Regulatory](Regulatory/) | QA/CSV teams, auditors, compliance officers, regulated engineering orgs | [trialmcp_regulatory.pdf](Regulatory/trialmcp_regulatory.pdf) |
| **C** | [Interoperability](Interoperability/) | Health IT architects, interoperability engineers, standards contributors (HL7/IHE/DICOM) | [trialmcp_interop.pdf](Interoperability/trialmcp_interop.pdf) |

## Combined PDF

[TrialMCP_Combined.pdf](TrialMCP_Combined.pdf) contains all three papers in order (A, B, C) with original page numbering (1-10, 1-10, 1-10).
